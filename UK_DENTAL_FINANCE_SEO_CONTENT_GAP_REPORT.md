# UK Dental Finance SEO Content-Gap Report

## Validation Summary
- `npm test`: ✅ passed
- `npm run lint`: ✅ passed
- `npm run build`: ✅ passed (33 static pages generated, including all `[slug]` routes)

## Current Coverage Snapshot
The site currently covers core transactional and informational clusters:
- Core finance terms (`dental-finance-uk`, `dental-payment-plans`, `0-percent-dental-finance`, `bad-credit-dental-finance`)
- Treatment-specific finance (`dental-implants-finance`, `veneers-finance`, Turkey-related pages)
- Calculators (`finance-calculator`, implant/veneer variants)
- Comparison pages (credit card, personal loan, UK vs Turkey costs)
- Trust/policy framework (editorial, medical review, privacy, terms, cookies, authors, contact)

## Key Content Gaps (Prioritized)

### 1) High-intent service + location pages (High)
Missing city/region modifiers that capture local transactional demand (e.g., London, Manchester, Birmingham, Glasgow, Leeds).  
**Gap impact:** lost opportunity for geo-qualified conversion traffic.

### 2) Procedure-cost + finance combinations (High)
Current content is broad; deeper pages for major procedures are missing (e.g., Invisalign finance, composite bonding finance, root canal finance, crowns finance, braces finance).  
**Gap impact:** weaker coverage of long-tail, treatment-specific commercial queries.

### 3) Eligibility scenario pages (High)
No dedicated pages for common lender decision contexts (self-employed, low income, no guarantor, soft-search-only, declined applications).  
**Gap impact:** missed “can I get dental finance if…” query class with high engagement potential.

### 4) NHS vs private decision content (Medium)
The site references NHS context but lacks dedicated decision pages (NHS wait times vs private finance, mixed treatment journeys, private top-up scenarios).  
**Gap impact:** reduced topical authority on UK patient pathways.

### 5) Conversion-support tools and templates (Medium)
Only payment calculators are present; no downloadable/interactive buyer aids (affordability checklist, lender comparison worksheet, pre-application checklist).  
**Gap impact:** weaker conversion assistance and lower return visits.

### 6) Rich editorial freshness layer (Medium)
No visible recurring “rates/market update” or frequently refreshed trend pages.  
**Gap impact:** lower freshness signals for SERP competitiveness in YMYL finance topics.

### 7) Stronger E-E-A-T evidence blocks (Medium)
Policies exist, but deeper author/reviewer entity pages and citation-level outbound linking are limited.  
**Gap impact:** constrained trust differentiation in medical-finance YMYL SERPs.

### 8) Internal linking by intent stage (Medium)
Internal links exist but are mostly generic; limited funnel mapping across awareness → comparison → calculator → lead actions.  
**Gap impact:** weaker crawl path optimization and conversion journey cohesion.

## Recommended Next Content Batches
1. **Local transaction layer:** top 10 UK city landing pages for dental finance.
2. **Procedure finance layer:** 8–12 high-volume treatment+finance pages.
3. **Eligibility layer:** scenario pages for common approval concerns.
4. **Trust + evidence layer:** expanded medical reviewer bios, citation-backed references, and update log pages.
5. **Funnel UX layer:** intent-based internal linking and conversion-assist templates.
